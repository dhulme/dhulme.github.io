Southampton Christian Union Media

9th February 2013

Over the past year I’ve had the opportunity to become more involved with media production at Southampton University Christian Union. As well as gaining better understanding of Adobe’s Creative Suite, I’ve been able to collaborate on several projects with my great friend Dave Betts composing the accompanying music to the videos.

One of these collaborative projects was a video to promote a week of events put on by the CU entitled ‘Uncover’. The events week video was interesting to work on as it built upon existing ideas from UCCF, I was able to use their original footage, and add elements to created a promo video suitable for our specific events week.

I’ve also recently produced a promotional video for Alpha. Creating this was entirely different with the inspiration coming from a video produced for Kerith Community Church a while ago. I used the same music, and produced something with a similar style and theme.
